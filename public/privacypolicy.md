## Introduction

Callaback ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our contact form and API documentation services.

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Usage Data:** IP address, browser type, access times, and pages viewed
- **Analytics:** We may use cookies and similar technologies to improve our services

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Send newsletters (if subscribed)

- Comply with legal obligations

Your information is:
- **Encrypted:** Protected by Cloudflare's security infrastructure
- **Limited Access:** Only accessible to authorized personnel
- **Not Sold:** We never sell your personal information to third parties

## Third-Party Services

We use:
- **Cloudflare:** For hosting, security, and performance
- **Analytics Tools:** To understand usage patterns (anonymized data)

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal information
- Request correction or deletion of your data
- Opt-out of newsletter communications
- Withdraw consent at any time

## Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law.
